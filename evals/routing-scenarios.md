# Product Composer Routing Scenarios

These scenarios record the initial active-reference window. `verification.md` belongs to the later verification stage except when validation-only is the entry intent, as in R10. R3, R4, R6, R7, R10, and R11 close the initial owner window before opening a later Asset truth window because missing or inaccessible media materially constrains proof, claims, accessibility, or composition. The later window carries the complete Design Contract forward and does not change the selected Phase or Surface.
